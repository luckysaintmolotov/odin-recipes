Spicy Cheesy Burrito

  • Home

Description:

The Spicy Cheesy Burrito is a delightful fusion of taste and texture, featuring a savory filling of ground beef or turkey, black beans, and fluffy rice, all enveloped in a warm flour tortilla. Enhanced with zesty salsa and taco seasoning, this dish offers a perfect balance of spice and creaminess, thanks to the generous amount of melted cheese. For those who crave an extra kick, sliced jalapeños can be added, making it customizable to your heat preference. Serve it with guacamole and sour cream for a complete meal that’s as delicious as it is satisfying. Whether you're cooking for family or hosting friends, these burritos are sure to be a hit!

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef or turkey
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup cooked rice (white or brown)
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or pepper jack)
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • 1 tablespoon taco seasoning
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 1 jalapeño, sliced (optional for extra spice)
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
  • Guacamole and sour cream (for serving)

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef or turkey over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add the black beans, cooked rice, salsa, taco seasoning, and jalapeño (if using) to the skillet. Stir until well combined and heated through.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in half of the shredded cheese.
  4. Warm the tortillas in a separate pan or microwave until pliable.
  5. To assemble, place a generous amount of the filling in the center of each tortilla. Sprinkle with the remaining cheese.
  6. Fold in the sides of the tortilla and roll it up tightly from the bottom. Place seam-side down on a plate.
  7. Optional: Toast the burritos in a skillet for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown and crispy.
  8. Serve with guacamole, sour cream, and garnish with fresh cilantro.
